📣Y9 Mission launch📣
The Year Nine “Who am I now?” mission has now been launched with students.
This mission is about celebrating the end of Key Stage 3 and reflecting upon achievements before they begin Key Stage 4 (GCSE’s) in Year 10.
This mission will celebrate success and encourage students to reflect on their achievements, big or small, and remind them of their growth since year seven.
They will be encouraged to reflect on their inner strengths they have developed over three years and consider moments of resilience, courage, or adaptability.
These are all key skills needed in many post 16 training, education or work opportunities.
Students will produce a year book and a short presentation to celebrate their accomplishments.
We can’t wait to see the end result! 1 Likes

📣Y7 Mission launch📣
The Year Seven “Who has the Power” mission has now been launched with students.
This mission encourages students to think about the different types of power, what it means to have power and how power can be used to create change in the world, promoting
🌎An awareness of Global issues – understanding the different types of power, the roles of power holders and why this matters.
🔍 Research skills and critical thinking - to find and research real examples of power in the complex world we live in
💬 Creativity, teamwork and communication - working as a team & sharing ideas to complete the mission.
The students will explore types of power, such as the power of words, nature, individuals or groups; they will then create an e book about Who Has The Power!
Students in previous years have done fantastic work on this mission and we can’t wait to see how this year’s forms get on! 1 Likes
