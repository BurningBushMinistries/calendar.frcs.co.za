Admissions – September 2025

We understand that choosing a high school can be a difficult decision. If you would like to discuss changing your child’s preference, please don’t hesitate to give us a call on 01257 400990.

The process is simple and we will be happy to assist you.

We are all happy and enthusiastic and ready to share the successful learning journey that pupils make at Shevington High School.

We are really looking forward to meeting you soon.
